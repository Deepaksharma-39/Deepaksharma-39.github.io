Introduction

Hello, I am Deepak Sharma,

An Aspiring full-stack web Developer, currently enrolled in Masai coding school to upskill my knowledge of programming languages and enter the world of technology.

I like immersive movie experiences, reading manga, and watching and playing football. Throughout my life, I have been involved in physical activity be it sports or just playing games. I like to stay fit and in good shape. I also started a football Academy after my graduation due to my interest and went ahead on a year-long journey into establishing a first-of-its-kind football academy in Bhopal (M.P). During the pandemic, I joined the local city council to help my town and was shortly offered to work there as a Hygiene Supervisor. When the Pandemic restrictions were lifted, I was ready to take another step.

Being the tech head of my family and seeing the endless opportunities in a field which encourages you to be creative, tech world seemed an obvious choice for me. So after careful consideration about what my first language needs to be and how should I start, I chose Masai school because it promised to upskill me in the fields that I was lacking and after spending almost 6 months at Masai I am pretty confident I made the right choice.
